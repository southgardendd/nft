STOP WAR IN UKRAINE

Ukrainians will never forget the events of February 24, 2022. All residents of Ukraine woke up at 5 AM local time. Someone from the explosion near, others from calls from parents and friends. On this day, Russia declared a "military operation", but in reality it is a war. On the first day, missile strikes were carried out on military facilities in many cities in Ukraine. For several days, the Russian army tried to invade the cities. after unsuccessful attempts, rocket attacks began on civilian homes, schools and hospitals.

Southgarden in collaboration with Fvckshot made music video NFT to support people who are in a difficult situation.

All the money raised from NFT sales will go to provide for people who have been forced to flee their homes.



OUR MISSON

Our project aims to raise money to provide humanitarian assistance to Ukrainians who fled their homes after the attack on their homes by the Russian army.

Our NFT shows the horrors of war - the death of civilians, propaganda and censorship, intimidation and infringement of human rights. But the ultimate goal of every Ukrainian is the independence of the country and a peaceful sky overhead.
